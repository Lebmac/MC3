A throttle twist away from history.

Follow the scent of roast coffee and oil to find a collection of over 60 motorcycles, spanning seven decades of riding heritage, from the polished chrome of the 1950s to the race-bred lines of the modern era. Each machine tells a story of craftsmanship, innovation, or the people who rode them.

Each mark on the tank has a story. Each machine, a journey.

The collection was built not just to display bikes, but to keep their spirit alive — engines that were meant to run, not sit silent. Visitors can wander between machines that once roared down open roads, then sit back with a coffee and take in the sound, smell, and soul of motorcycling's golden years.

We're sparked by stories and the echo of engines.

This space celebrates the connection between people, machines, and moments. It's a tribute to the riders who shaped motorcycling's past and the ones still writing its future.

In need of a Kickstart ?

Whether you’re reviving a long-sleeping classic or hunting for that elusive part, our dismantling bay is where forgotten bikes find new life. Each machine is carefully stripped, catalogued, and stored — ready to give another rider the spark they need to get rolling again. From engines to emblems, every piece still has a story to tell.

From Rust to Radiance .

A careful hand, a soft cloth, and a lot of pride - that’s how we detail. We’ll chase the grime from every groove so your bike leaves looking sharper, smoother, and ready to turn heads again.
